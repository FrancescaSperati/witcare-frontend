​Access and Support Program (A&S)

The Access and Support Program is a free service that offers short-term individual support to eligible individuals of Italian descent to understand, access and negotiate the range of services available through the Commonwealth Home Support Program.

As part of this service, Co.As.It:

  • Provides information on the Commonwealth Home Support Program and community support services available to older Italians living in Victoria.
  • Offers assistance in contacting the My Aged Care Contact Centre.
  • Provides support throughout the registration and eligibility verification process.
  • Offers assistance in dealing with the different teams in charge of assessing the application.
  • Works in collaboration with other agencies involved with the client including family, friends, doctors and other health professionals.


What local government areas are covered by the A&S program
The program covers specific local government areas in the Northern and Western suburbs of Melbourne. Specifically, the local government areas covered in the Northern suburbs are:

  • City of Moreland
  • City of Darebin
  • City of Yarra
  • City of Hume
  • City of Melbourne

The local government area covered in the Western suburb is:

  • City of Moonee Valley


The Main Function and Personnel of the A&S program
The Access and Support program in the North Western region of Melbourne is managed by Stella Tallorito who is fundamental in accessing the programs at CoAsIt. The Access and Support program is not a specific full case management service, but can provide follow up services after the assistance provided during the initial assessment. Once an Aged Care assessment has been made, the client will receive a code that can be used at one of the facilities selected and considered suitable for the type of assistance established by the MyAgedCare evaluation team. Once the service has been selected, the Commonwealth Home Support Program (CHSP) will finance the project. This is usually one of the stages in the process where a follow-up service is necessary
